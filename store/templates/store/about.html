Behind the Opera Home Signature

Opera Home is a design-driven studio creating modern, durable home essentials with a distinctive black-and-gold signature. Every piece is built to feel as good as it looks—day after day.

Philosophy

Quiet Luxury, Every Day

We believe luxury is not loud logos—it’s thoughtful details, balanced proportions, and products that age beautifully in real homes.

Craft

Materials That Endure

From powder-coated metals to high-density fabrics, every material is selected for long-term performance, easy care, and a refined finish.

Experience

Designed Around You

Clear specs, honest photography, and responsive support—so you can choose the right piece once and enjoy it for years.

Our Studio Story

Opera Home began with a simple question: what if everyday objects felt like they belonged in a boutique hotel, without the boutique price? Our designers and engineers work together to remove visual noise, refine proportions, and obsess over the touches you feel every single day.

We prototype in small batches, test in real homes, and only release what meets our standards for comfort, durability, and timeless appeal.
