Design-led pieces for calm, modern homes

Opera Home creates furniture, lighting and decor that feel like a boutique hotel, but live comfortably in everyday apartments and houses.

  • Minimal silhouettes with warm, quiet colours.
  • Pieces that are easy to style, move and layer.
  • Subtle black-and-gold accents as our signature detail.
Philosophy

Quiet luxury, not noise

We replace heavy branding with simple forms, soft textures and clean lines. Your rooms feel calm and elevated, not crowded or over-styled.

Craft

Built for real life

Every finish, fabric and handle is chosen to survive daily use while still looking refined: easy-to-clean surfaces, comfortable proportions, solid details.

Experience

Design help, not just products

Honest photography, clear dimensions and styling guidance help you build a complete corner, not just buy a single object and hope it fits.

From empty rooms to composed spaces

Opera Home started with bare apartments that felt flat and unfinished. The question was simple: what if a few well-chosen pieces could change the mood of an entire room? Each collection we design is an answer to that question.

Our team sketches, prototypes and tests in real homes. Anything that feels unnecessary is removed. What remains are confident, easy-to-combine pieces that stay relevant as your life and space change.

How we build a collection

  • Start with a mood: evening light, a colour story, a type of gathering at home.
  • Define a small family of core pieces: seating, tables, lighting and key decor.
  • Design supporting objects that layer in without visual noise.
  • Style everything together so you can recreate the look with just a few items.

The result: rooms that feel curated, warm and lived-in, never like a crowded showroom.

Made for the way you really live

Whether you are furnishing your first apartment or refreshing a long-loved home, Opera Home is designed to give you fewer, better choices. Mix a single statement piece into what you already own or build an entire room from our signature palette— either way, your space should feel intentional, calm and unmistakably yours.
